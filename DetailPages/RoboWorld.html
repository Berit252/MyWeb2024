Ramadan Manual

这组手册是从一个原创的虚构未来机器世界的角度编写的。我希望通过它让人们一睹这个未来文明，同时也鼓励观众反思我们的社会以及人与机器(AI)之间复杂的矛盾关系。
This set of manuals is written from the perspective of a fictional futuristic machine world. Through it, I hope to provide people a glimpse into this future civilization, while also encouraging the audience to reflect on our society and the complex relationship between humans and machines.



12.2023.
