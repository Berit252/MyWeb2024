Simulation & Dreams
- Dream Frames

新媒体/技术的离散化特性允许以多种方式操作和重组数据，从而导致同一组数字组件的不同输出 —— 就像记忆和思想的碎片如何形成不同的梦境一样。
The discrete nature of new media and technology allows for the manipulation and reassembly of data in myriad ways, leading to diverse outputs from the same set of digital components—similar to how dreams can form from fragmented memories and thoughts.

我受到跨学科合作者Dr. Makayla Lewis的项目（该项目通过梦境探索我们人类与电子设备的关系）的启发，捕捉并勾画了我的一个关于手机的梦境。此后又使用建模软件，将这个梦境重构成动画。
Inspired by Dr. Makayla Lewis's project, which explores our relationship with electronic devices through dreams, I captured and sketched one of my dreams involving a mobile phone. Using modeling software, this dream was recreated as a short animation.

为了表达梦境的离散与模糊，我将这些渲染出来的动画画面转印到了3D打印的平面上。通过这一个个的小气泡切片，我将新媒体设备和梦境共有的离散性带到观众眼前，并邀请所有人共同参与这个探究的过程。
To express the fragmented and ephemeral quality of dreams, I transferred these animated frames onto 3D printed planes, creating a series of chips that highlight the discrete and blurred aspects of new media and dreams. The audiences are invited to engage with and reflect on the process of information assembly in our digital age. And I encourage everyone to participate in this exploration and share your own experiences with technology and dreams.
